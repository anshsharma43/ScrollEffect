RABBITS

Rabbits, or bunnies, are small mammals in the family Leporidae.
Male rabbits are called bucks females are called does .

CATS

The cat is a domestic species of small carnivorous mammal.
Cats are one of the most popular domesticated species.
Domestic cats are valued by humans for companionship and their ability to hunt rodents .

DUCKS

Duck is the common name for numerous species of waterfowl in the family Anatidae .
Ducks are generally smaller and shorter-necked than swans and geese, which are also members of the same family.

KITTENS

A kitten is a juvenile cat .
After being born, kittens display primary altriciality and are totally dependent on their mother for survival.
They do not normally open their eyes until after seven to ten days.
